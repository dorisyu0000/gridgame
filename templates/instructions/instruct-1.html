Welcome to the Grid World Experiment!

In this experiment, you will explore a grid world by revealing hidden symbols. Here's how to play:

Starting Position

You start with one revealed +1 symbol.
Grey squares are hidden symbols.

Gameplay

Move your mouse and click to reveal symbols.
Find all +1 symbols to complete the trial.

Scoring System

+1 point
Correct symbol
-1 point
Incorrect symbol

Goal: Find all +1 symbols while avoiding -1 symbols
to maximize your score

  • Each trial begins with one revealed +1 symbol.
  • Move your mouse and click grey squares to reveal hidden symbols.
  • Your goal is to find all +1 symbols while revealing as few -1 symbols as possible.
  • The trial ends when you find all +1 symbols.
  • You will complete 120 trials in total, with breaks every 10 trials.
  • For every 100 points you earn, you will receive a $1 bonus on your base payment!
