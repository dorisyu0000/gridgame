How to Play

During Each Trial:

  • Start with 6 points at the beginning of each trial
  • Click to reveal hidden symbols:
    • Finding a +1 symbol: Keep your points
    • Finding a -1 symbol: Lose one point
  • Trial ends when you find all +1 symbols

Score Example:

Current Trial: 2/120
Score: 6
Cumulative Score: 3

Tips:

  • Your score carries over between trials
  • Try to find +1 symbols efficiently
  • Avoid clicking on -1 symbols when possible
  • Take your time to think about where to click
  • You'll get breaks every 10 trials to rest
